FAQ

  • Where can I find a midi editor?

    Download a free midi editor like MuseScore which you can find here.

    • What melodies work the best?

    As Prelude49 is in alpha, it is only optimized for certain melodies.

    Make sure your melody follows these guidelines:

    • Tranpose your melody to C Major or A minor
    • The melody should be monophonic (no chords)
    • Use a 4/4 or 2/4 time signature
    • Do not use note durations shorter than 16th notes (i.e. 32nd, 64th)
    • Do not use grace notes
    • Do not use triplets or other tuplets
    • Do not include a pick-up bar (i.e. do not start the song with a bar that is mainly rests)
